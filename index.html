Welcome to our new project, a new book. It's tentatively called, "Geeks: For Non-Geeks" and will be a collections of "geeks" answers to questions like who was your role model growing up and why? etc

The purpose of the book?
Two-fold really,
  1. to give people who don't understand geeks a better insight
  2. to give geeks, particularly those who are younger, a feeling of belonging
Shortly, we'll have information here on how you can contribute to the book. I know I felt isolated growing up being a geek and felt it was time to do something about it. We won't be able to take every answer from everyone, but would like to take the most interesting ones and publish it as a book both in e-format and physical paperback.
